what is web design

Web design is the process of planning, creating, and arranging the visual elements and user experience of a website. It encompasses both the aesthetic aspects, like colors and layout, and the functional aspects, like navigation and user interface, to ensure a user-friendly and engaging online experience. Essentially, it's the art and science of crafting how a website looks, feels, and functions
